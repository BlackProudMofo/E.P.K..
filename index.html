Profile

Honest Ornan Okoawo (Krexx Nv) is a Nigerian Afro-fusion artist from Edo State. Active since 2012, his work blends Afrobeats, Hip-Hop, and R&B with precise writing and lived-city realism.

After a label dispute pulled his catalog from DSPs, he rebuilt independently and founded Nimble Nova Entertainment—resulting in a tighter catalog, sharper voice, and full creative control.

Recent projects include INTEL (Inside The Eko Life) and the EP Body Mind & Soul.
